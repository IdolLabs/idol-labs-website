Battleseed Badger

Release year: 2020

Original App Store Description:

BLAST into battle with Battleseed Badger! This brand new action game sees you, the titular Battleseed Badger soaring into the skies as you face off against WORMS in superpowered spacecraft. The only thing that can stop these tech-savvy worms is good ol' fashion fruit seeds. Eat an apple and spit the seeds at worms to destroy their spacecraft! Can you SAVE THE WORLD and get a HIGH SCORE? That mission is in your hands!

Battleseed Badger is the debut game from Idol Labs. The game features:

-Several enemy types varying in difficulty to dodge and defeat

- A dynamic music soundtrack

-Accelerometer controls with sensitivity adjustment

-A shop featuring upgrades and power-ups

Notes:

The game was eventually delisted from the the Apple App Store and Google Play Store due to lack of ongoing support.
