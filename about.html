About

Welcome to Idol Labs! My name is Andrew Dauphinais and I use this company as the place to publish various software projects that I have determined are actually worth sharing, as opposed to the ones collecting dust on my GitHub.

I intentionally came up with the most generic software company name I could think of that I could still purchase the domain for. Hence, Idol Labs was born.

Originally, Idol Labs was a mobile game development company. The first game released was Battleseed Badger in 2020. Since then, my interests in software development have evolved and Battleseed Badger has since been delisted.

I’m excited to launch Rhyme Offline. Rhyme Offline isn’t just the best Rhyming Dictionary app on the market, featuring not only perfect rhymes, but slant rhymes, definitions, a thesaurus, related words, and more. Unlike most app, it works offline, in airplane mode and it’s 100% free with ZERO ads.

So if this is a company, how are you making money? This isn’t my day job, and, at least in the case of Rhyme Offline, that’s just a project of passion. If you enjoy Rhyme Offline and wanna tip in for some lunch, you can do so here.
